Lightweight IT Architecture, Open by Nature, Designed for Enterprises

At Sihua Tech, together with our affiliated sibling company Shanghai-based KXTech, We’re building software to help people easily create medium to large scale enterprise cloud IT infrastructure in their own data center. We delivers what modem enterprises crave: lightweight IT architecture, enterprise-class performance, operational and maintenance functionality, with the cloud economics business demand.
The company’s software-driven XOS, Express VM and Megabric SDS suites natively converges computer, virtualization and storage into x86 server form factor. The result is lots of functionality in a much smaller footprint and the elimination of independent device management. Using SHTech, customers benefit from predictable performance, linear stability and cloud-like infrastructure consumption, elevating IT to focus on the applications and services that power their business.
